Sixty Hertz Engineering

Electrical engineering consulting firm with expertise in industrial and commercial power distribution, the design of components such as panel boards, lighting, motor control and electrical protection, and power system analysis including arc flash. Lawrence Charles Hale is the principal engineer with over 35 years of engineering experience.

  • Learn more

Lawrence Charles Hale

Lawrence Hale has worked on a broad spectrum of projects for the oil and gas industry, agricultural facilities, retail stores, post offices, US Public Health Service water and wastewater treatment facilities, refineries, recreation and public works. Project locations include rural Alaska villages, the North Slope, Fairbanks, Anchorage, California, NY, Idaho, Oregon, Washington, Iraq and Afghanistan.

Lawrence has designed power studies including flash and short circuit calculations including arc flash labels, and heat tracing for cold regions such as the North Slope. He has specified diesel and micro turbine generators and turbine generator balance of plant, transfer switches and installation, and integrated switch gear. He provides project construction cost estimating, professional engineering opinions and detailed bills of materials, construction observation service, and Fire Marshal and utility company coordination.

Lawrence is well versed in the National Electrical Code, the Standard for Electrical Safety in the Workplace, hazardous areas, including both division and zone systems. He has served as Lead Electrical and Manager of Electrical, Instrument and Control Departments. Lawrence has worked in difficult, challenging environments that have included the North Slope, Iraq and Afghanistan. He received training in survival skills in freezing temperatures and learned the dangers of working in hot, risky war zones. Lawrence is dedicated, thorough and believes in timely work, meeting scheduled deadlines and supporting projects through construction that extends into evenings and weekends.
