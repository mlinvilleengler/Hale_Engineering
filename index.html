Lawrence Charles Hale

The Principal at Hale Engineering, Larry Hale, has over 35 years of engineering experience. He has worked on a broad spectrum of projects that cover the oil and gas industry, power distribution, lighting (inside and out), agricultural facilities, stores, post offices, water and wastewater facilities, power studies including arc flash labels, heat tracing, US Public Health Service water and wastewater in rural Alaska villages, refineries, test facilities and engineering for cold regions such as the North Slope. Mr. Hale also has leadership skills through such positions as Lead Electrical Engineer and Manager of an Electrical and I & C Department. He keeps up with changing codes such as the National Electrical Code, the Standard for Electrical Safety in the Workplace and hazardous areas including both the division and zone systems. Mr. Hale believes that living in Alaska has been the key to obtaining experience in such a wide range of projects, and he enjoys challenges and working with people. Mr. Hale also believes in timely work, meeting deadlines, supporting projects through construction during all hours including evenings and weekends. He is focused and is dedicated to thoroughness and exceptional work.
